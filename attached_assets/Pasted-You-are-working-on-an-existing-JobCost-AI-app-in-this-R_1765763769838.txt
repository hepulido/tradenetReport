4) Multi-tenant: can it handle multiple companies?
What “multi-tenant” means in your current setup
With your schema (companies/projects/transactions/etc.), the simplest multi-tenant mode is:

One login/user (even no auth)

Multiple companies

Every record has company_id

Every API call requires companyId and filters by it

That’s totally fine for “you + family” or early pilots.

What can go wrong if it’s NOT enforced
If any endpoint loads by projectId or reportId without checking companyId, you can leak data across companies. That’s why the prompt adds guards.
